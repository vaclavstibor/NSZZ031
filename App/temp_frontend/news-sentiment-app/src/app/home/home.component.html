INTERESTED IN EXPLORING NEWS SENTIMENT?

We provide a platform for analysing news sentiment data and its impact on stock market trends. Our platform offers a comprehensive overview of news sentiment data, enabling the exploration of the correlation between articles and companies and visualising sentiment trends over time.

Learn more

GRAPH PAGE

Each company is represented within a graph, where nodes corresponding to articles and the company share the same size. The colour of each article node and its connecting edge reflects the company's sentiment in the article, detailed in the control panel. The company is coloured blue.

GRAPHS PAGE

The graph visualises relationships between articles and the companies mentioned within them. Node sizes are determined by relevance: for company nodes, size correlates with the frequency of article mentions, while for article nodes, it reflects the count of mentioned companies.

Article nodes also display the average sentiment of all referenced companies, influencing their colour. Company nodes are uniformly coloured blue. Additionally, the colour of the connecting edges mirrors the sentiment of the associated entity in the article, providing a comprehensive sentiment overview at a glance.

DATA SOURCE

The Guardian is a British news and media website owned by the Guardian Media Group. It contains nearly all of the content of the newspapers The Guardian and The Observer, as well as a substantial body of web-only work produced by its own staff, including a rolling news service.

Through the use of Open Platform, we have free access to and can analyze news articles whole content from The Guardian, offering a valuable data source.
